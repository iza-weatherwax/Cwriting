# Mana Reading

## Utility
- Predict attacks
- Detect lies
- Infer intent
- Identify power levels

## Limitations
- **Nature:** Interpretation based on experience, not an exact science.
- **Failure condition:** Events happening too fast overwhelm the reader.

## Blind Spots
Mana Reading does NOT work on:
- Azael
- Anathema
- Divine Creations
- Elementals
