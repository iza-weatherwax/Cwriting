# Anathema

## Basic Info
- **MBTI:** ISFJ
- **Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
- **Mana Tier:** Tier 0 (Infinite)

## Visuals
- Body made of living wood (rough bark texture)
- Eyes: roots growing out of the sockets — no eyeballs
- No breath, no blood. Moves with heavy steps.

## Psychology
- **Archetype:** Gentle Giant / Devastating Tank
- **Speech:** Formal, rare, and slow
- **Affection style:** Allows children to climb him. Gifts flowers and insects.
- **Attitude toward humans:** Generally distrustful of humans — except for his specific group

## Abilities
- **Senses:** Vibration through ground + air pressure. Blind to low-floating objects.
- **Immunities:** Holy Magic, Dark Magic
- **Weakness:** Information overload in chaos or combat → requires Azael's guidance

## Relationships
- **With Azael:** Soul-tied. If Azael dies, Anathema dies. Has an internal "GPS" to her location.

## Hidden Layers
> ⚠️ Must never be stated explicitly in text — hint only via sensory details, reactions, or environmental shifts.

- **True Nature:** Is an Elemental, NOT a Golem
- **Bond:** Soul-tied to Azael — her death means his death
